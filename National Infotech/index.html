About Us

National Infotech is proud of its experienced faculty and producing many scholars. National Infotech has received an increasing number of awards and is home to hundred’s of awards across the teaching excellence, citations, and programme categories.

Our History

One of Terai's leading IT institutions, National Infotech College established in B.S 2062. is a cerebral destination for students across the region and in Birgunj in particular. Its illustrious history of developing talent is what has inspired generations of students to be the best they can be. The college offers a conducive environment for learning, be it academically or in extracurricular activities. Known for its emphasis on education, entrepreneurship, research, and sports, National Infotech College has some of the best minds in the educational and research fields who work together to inspire groundbreaking work.

Core Value

At National Infotech College, we believe in ethics and integrity, the scientific temper, environmental consciousness and sustainable development, promotion of Nepalese culture and heritage, active citizenry, and service to society.

Vision

At National Infotech College, we believe that a quality education is the key to unlocking the potential of students, and we're dedicated to providing students with the best possible learning experience. We believe that education should be about more than just filling kids' heads with facts. It should prepare them for the real world—and give them the confidence and skills they need to succeed. That's why our focus is on fostering human development through excellence in Quality Education, Research, and Entrepreneurial Development.

Mission

At Nation Infotech, we believe that every person is capable of greatness. We want you to feel empowered to be your best self, to reach for the stars and strive for success in whatever it is you do. We want you to feel like you can make a difference in the world, and we want you to know that you matter. We're here to help you do just that by providing quality education, creating human assets / manpower and intellectual capital, enhancing research and development in different disciplines, developing new generation entrepreneurs who will be instrumental in fueling economic growth, creating able Leaders, Managers, and Technocrats—you name it! We want our students to be prepared for whatever comes their way as they embark on their own paths of success. Because every person has a story worth telling—and we can't wait to hear yours!

MESSAGE FROM PRINCIPAL

We extend our warmest welcome to you at National Infotech where we started our journey in the pursuit of educational excellence in B.S 2062. It has been a wonderful experience so far; one that can be cherished by all associated with this institution: the students and the teachers. The entire gamut of activities at NI reflects its objective of nurturing young minds and helping them grow as responsible citizen who can make positive contribution to the society and the nation.
